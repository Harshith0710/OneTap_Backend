Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the QuickTap AI homepage. Enter your details such as name, email, and password to complete the registration process.

2. How can I reset my password?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. You'll be asked to provide your registered email address to receive a password reset link.

3. How do I subscribe to QuickTap AI services?

You can subscribe to QuickTap AI by selecting a subscription plan from our Pricing page and following the checkout instructions. We offer monthly and annual subscription options.

4. Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings page. Please note that cancellations will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

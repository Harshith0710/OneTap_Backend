Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 11, 2025

Entity: ZiraMinds AI Pvt. Ltd.

Application: QuickTap AI (Android)

1. Introduction

ZiraMinds AI Pvt. Ltd. (“ZiraMinds”, “Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the nature, scope, purpose, and legal basis for the collection, processing, and protection of personal and non-personal data in connection with the mobile application QuickTap AI (“App”).

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies solely to the QuickTap AI Android application and governs the data practices regarding user interactions, embedded third-party services, and device-level permissions utilized by the App.

3. Data Collection & Processing

3.1. Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

QuickTap AI does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information unless explicitly provided by the user during authentication via Firebase.

3.2. Authentication Data

We integrate Firebase Authentication, which may collect data such as:

  • Email address
  • Auth tokens
  • Anonymized Firebase User ID

Data is governed by Google's Firebase Privacy Notice.

3.3. Chat and Conversational Data

We use the Gemini API for AI chat responses. User prompts and AI responses are processed transiently and are not stored or logged.

3.4. Image Data & Media Access

Users can upload or capture images for AI analysis. These are processed locally and not stored, transmitted, or retained.

3.5. Voice Interaction Data

Voice input is processed via Google Text-to-Speech (gTTS). No voice recordings are stored or transmitted.

4. Third-Party Technologies

The App integrates:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Google AdMob
  • Gemini API
  • gTTS (Google Text-to-Speech)

Each provider has its own privacy policy and may collect device-level data independently.

5. Advertising & Monetization

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect data such as:

  • Device ID (AAID)
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Ad and app usage metrics

Ad personalization is subject to Google's Advertising Policies. Consent is obtained where required (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

6. Data Storage & Retention

We do not store user data externally. Local preferences are stored using SQLite or JSON and are cleared upon reinstallation or cache clearance.

7. Data Security

We use security measures such as:

  • Secure token handling
  • Encrypted API communication (TLS)
  • Minimal data footprint

8. Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Legal Basis for Processing

We process data under the following bases:

  • User consent
  • Legitimate interest
  • Legal compliance
